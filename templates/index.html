About us..

A health analysis prediction web app is a digital tool designed to help users assess their risk of developing various health conditions. This application is used to predict the current medical state of an individual regarding the three most common diseases. The diseases are heart disease, Pneumonia, covid 19. A health analysis prediction website aims to empower individuals to take control of their health and make informed decisions about their well-being. Providing actionable insights and personalized recommendations can help users make positive changes that lead to improved health outcomes and a higher quality of life.
